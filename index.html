Who we are.

We are a team of data scientists from a diverse set of backgrounds who came together to leverage data science, machine learning, and artificial intelligence to address epidemic health problems. Our aim is to understand and predict the effects and the causes of health epidemics, and determine solutions to minimize the impact and harm of epidemics.
